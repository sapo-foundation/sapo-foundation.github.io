# Delete your SAPO account
To delete your SAPO account, open the SAPO app and go to:
**Settings → Data Controls → Delete account**

If you opened this page from a deletion email and SAPO is installed, try opening the link from your email again.

If you cannot access the app or your account, email:
**support@sapo.surf**

Please include the email address associated with your SAPO account. We may need to verify your identity before deleting account data.
